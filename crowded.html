CROWDED PLACES YOU CAN VISIT
1.
Charminar_Bazaar

Buyers interested in buying some of the most precious and exquisite pearls, especially Basra pearl, must visit the Charminar Bazaar which is one of the most stylish options for street shopping in Hyderabad. These pearls may also be embellished with silver or gold as per your own preference. Moreover, home to some top-notch stores for clothing, you can choose from a plethora of ethnic sarees, traditional Hyderabadi khada dupatta, sherwanis and dress materials.

Hyderabad is a city which will never cease to amaze you in all ways possible. Be it the architectural beauty, its rich heritage or the soothing beauty of the city all around, you are sure to fall in love with the city time and again. And with such great options for street shopping in Hyderabad, one cannot help but wish to visit the city to explore it to the fullest and witness its diversity.

Timings: 10 am to 9 pm
Popular for: Pearls, Accessories, Jewellery, Ethnic Clothing

2
Moazzam_Jahi_Market

If you are looking for some great deals on beautiful architectural pieces from the Nizam area, then this street shopping in Hyderabad is exactly where you need to go. Home to dozens and dozens of stalls selling fresh meat, vegetables, local perfume caller ittar and even the choicest of dry fruits; you will find it all at reasonable rates. Do not miss out on buying some savouries from the famous Karachi Bakery which can be found in this very market.

Timings: 10 am to 11:30 pm
Popular for: Brass Ware, Ikkat Weavers

3
Begum_Bazaar

True to its name, Begum Bazaar is indeed the queen of all markets for street shopping in Hyderabad. If you are a tobacco taker, then Begum Bazaar is bound to be your destination as it is home to one of the largest tobacco markets in the state. However, not just tobacco, Begum Bazaar is also quite renowned for a plethora of items sold here which includes kitchen utensils, household products, toiletries, perfumes, daily use items and cosmetics to begin with. Being in business for more than 150 years, this shopping hub is just the place for shopaholics.

Timings: 10 am to 11 pm
Popular for: Household Commodities, Metal Works, Bidriware
4
Nampally

Your street shopping in Hyderabad is incomplete if you do not visit the Nampally market. This market stocks loads and loads of bags, wallets, jewellery, bangles, clothes, footwear and purses and is quite renowned for the diverse merchandise sold here. The Numaish exhibition is held in the months of January and early February here with almost every product under the sun being sold at this extremely famous exhibition. With all the items being reasonably priced in this market, you are bound to end up with some great deals and products to take back home!

Timings: 10 am to 11 pm
Popular for: Electronics, Traditional Indian Wear, Kalamkari Fabric
